RULES

  1. There will be several levels with increasing difficulty.
  2. A player can only be in one team.
  3. One player will create the team and another member can join by entering the team code.
  4. Once you join/create a team you cannot leave.
  5. Answers to the questions are locations inside the NIT Durgapur campus.
  6. You cannot proceed to the next level without completing the current level.
  7. Hints will be released as per the number of teams stuck on a particular question.
  8. Join the official REChase whatsapp group to see hints.
  9. Seeing a hint won't cost any penalty.
  10. Intercommunication between teams are strongly discouraged.
  11. The team reaching the final destination first will be declared the winner.

Developed by: RECursion (Team Aavishkar)
